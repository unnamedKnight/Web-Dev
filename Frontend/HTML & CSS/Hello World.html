Pet Sitting Instructions

Dear Maria, thank you very much for volunteering to pet-sit for us. In this document you will find detailed care instructions for both of our pets.

Grizzly

Grizzly is our adorable yeloow lab puppy

Please, give Grizzly 2 1/2 cups of his kibble every day. Most importantly, make sure no matter how much he begs for or whines, never feed him extra food.

Acceptable Treats

  • Beacon
  • Chicken Breast
  • Cheese
    • Cheddar
    • Cream Cheese

Princess Popcorn

Princess Popcorn is our goldfish (Carassius auratus). Her tank is an absolute nightmare right now. We have to do something about it. Please, follow these cleaning instructions:

  1. Remove Princess Popcorn
  2. Clean the inside glass
  3. Siphon the water
  4. Clean the water
  5. Return Princess Popcorn to the tank
